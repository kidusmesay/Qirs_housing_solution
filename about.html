About QIRS Housing Solutions

Your trusted partner in finding the perfect home in Ethiopia

Our Story

QIRS Housing Solutions was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make finding and purchasing property in Ethiopia easier, more transparent, and more accessible for everyone. What started as a small team of passionate real estate professionals has grown into one of the most trusted property platforms in the country.

Our journey began when we noticed the challenges many Ethiopians faced when trying to find suitable housing. The lack of organized listings, transparent pricing, and reliable information made the process unnecessarily difficult. We set out to change that by creating a platform that connects property seekers with verified listings and trusted agents.

Our Mission

At QIRS Housing Solutions, our mission is to transform the real estate experience in Ethiopia by providing a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform that connects property seekers with their ideal homes. We strive to make the property search process simple, enjoyable, and successful for everyone.
